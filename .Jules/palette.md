## 2025-03-21 - Added Contextual Tooltips to Simulation Settings
**Learning:** Simulation dashboards often use domain-specific terms (like "Scout Ratio", "Ph Decay Rate", or "Wind Reset") which aren't immediately intuitive to all users. Adding simple `on_hover_text` in Bevy Egui provides excellent, non-intrusive contextual help.
**Action:** When working on complex interactive settings panels, add descriptive tooltips to sliders and buttons to explain their behavior without cluttering the UI.
